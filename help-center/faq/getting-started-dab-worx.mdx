## Quick Start

1. Sign up at `https://dabworx.com/register`.
2. Verify your email address.
3. Log in to your dashboard.
4. Click "New Project" to create a workspace.
5. Start adding documentation pages.

## Tips

- Use the sidebar to organize pages.
- Invite team members via the "Team" tab.
- Changes are saved automatically.
