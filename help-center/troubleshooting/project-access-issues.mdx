---
title: Can't Access My Project
description: Resolve project access problems in DAB Worx.
---

## Symptoms
- Loading spinner never stops
- "Project not found" message

## Possible Causes
- Wrong project URL
- Permissions issue

## Steps to Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Project URL">
    Make sure the link matches the one in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Access">
    Ask your admin to confirm your project permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    Email support@dabworx.com for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
